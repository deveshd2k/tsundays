RULES

A player will enter into a duo match with a moderator or an admin (Bot). The Bot will kill itself as soon as the match starts and will spectate the player. Each player will play 2 solo Duo matchs based on the given format. In case of a tie, the players with equal points will play a match each on given format and the one with more points wins. If it ties again, they keep on playing matches based on given format until one wins.

  • Victory Royale : +3 Points
  • 8+ Eliminations: +2 Points
  • 4+ Eliminations: +1 Point
  • Each Elimination: +1 Point
